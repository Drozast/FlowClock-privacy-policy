FlowClock Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 15, 2025
Application: FlowClock - HIIT & Tabata Timer
Developer: Damián Rozas

At FlowClock, we value and respect your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you use our application.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information you provide voluntarily

  • User account: Email address, username (optional)
  • Settings: App preferences, custom routines
  • Feedback: Comments and suggestions you send us

1.2 Information collected automatically

  • Usage data: Usage time, features used, workout frequency
  • Technical information: Device model, operating system, app version
  • Performance data: Crashes, technical errors, performance metrics
  • Unique identifiers: Device ID, installation ID

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide the service: Authentication, data synchronization, app functionality
  • Improve experience: Personalize interface, optimize performance
  • Technical support: Diagnose problems, resolve errors
  • Analytics: Understand usage patterns to improve the application
  • Communication: Important notifications, updates
  • Security: Detect and prevent fraud or malicious use

3. Third-Party Services

FlowClock integrates the following third-party services that may collect information:

3.1 Google Firebase

  • Purpose: Authentication, database, analytics
  • Data: Email, user ID, usage data
  • Policy: Google Privacy Policy

3.2 Google AdMob

  • Purpose: Display personalized ads
  • Data: Advertising ID, device information
  • Policy: AdMob Privacy

3.3 RevenueCat

  • Purpose: Subscription and purchase management
  • Data: User ID, subscription information
  • Policy: RevenueCat Privacy

4. Information Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for commercial purposes, except in the following circumstances:

  • With your explicit consent
  • To provide services you request
  • To comply with legal obligations
  • To protect our rights and security

5. Storage and Security

5.1 Where we store your data

  • Data stored on Google Firebase servers (United States)
  • Local data on your device
  • Subscription information on RevenueCat

5.2 Security measures

  • Encryption: All communications use HTTPS/TLS
  • Limited access: Only authorized personnel access data
  • Authentication: Secure authentication system
  • Monitoring: Continuous security monitoring

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Rectify: Correct inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Delete: Request deletion of your data
  • Portability: Obtain your data in portable format
  • Object: Object to processing in certain circumstances
  • Restrict: Request processing restriction

7. Data Retention

  • Active account: While you use the application
  • Deleted account: 30 days for backup, then permanent deletion
  • Crash data: Up to 90 days for diagnostics
  • Analytics data: Up to 26 months (anonymized)

8. Children

FlowClock is not directed at children under 13 years old. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we discover that we have collected information from a child under 13, we will delete it immediately.

9. Advertising and Cookies

9.1 Personalized advertising

  • AdMob may show ads based on your interests
  • You can disable personalization in your device settings
  • Premium users don't see ads

9.2 Tracking technologies

  • We use device identifiers for analytics
  • We don't use traditional web cookies
  • Firebase Analytics to understand app usage

10. International Transfers

Your data may be transferred and stored on servers located outside your country of residence, including the United States. These transfers are made with the following safeguards:

  • Compliance with appropriate transfer frameworks
  • Technical and organizational security measures
  • Data protection contracts with providers

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. We will notify you of significant changes through:

  • In-app notification
  • Email (if you have an account)
  • Updating the "last updated" date

12. Legal Bases (GDPR)

We process your personal data under the following legal bases:

  • Consent: For personalized advertising
  • Legitimate interest: For analytics and service improvement
  • Contract performance: To provide app services
  • Legal obligation: To comply with legal requirements
